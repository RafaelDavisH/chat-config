Rules:

Before you begin, we ask that you take 2 minutes to read our Code of Conduct which we enforce strictly across our community.

Additionally, we have a couple of rules specific for the Chat server:

1. Large blocks of code can be disruptive to the flow of chat. If you want to share more than a few lines, use a service like Pastebin or GitHub Gists and share the link instead.

2. Please refrain from uploading files directly (especially code files) for the safety of our users.

3. Discussions should be kept in the appropriate channels.

4. Please do not use the everyone or here mentions.

5. Please refrain from sharing links to social media accounts, blog posts, or YouTube videos as this is considered (self-)promotion. GitHub accounts and development projects for review (such as a Tribute Page) are acceptable.

6. Please refrain from discussing drugs, alcohol, or other content that may not be appropriate for our users under the age of 18.

If you have any questions, you are always welcome to reach out to one of the friendly moderators!

Thank you for joining us, and happy coding!
